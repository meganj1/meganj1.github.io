Some Intro to Horoscope

According to Wikipedia, the definition of horoscope is "a forecast of a person's future, typically including a delineation of character and circumstances, based on the relative positions of the stars and planets at the time of that person's birth." I think these forecasts are pretty accurate for me, or at least I feel relatable to those defining charactistics of my signs.

What we are going to look at today are my "big three." The big three are one's sun, moon, and rising sign. Let's take a look!

Sun in Leo

The Sun determines your ego, identity, and "role" in life. It's the core of who you are, and is the sign you’re most likely to already know. My sun in Leo, meaning I am fundemantally bold and proud. I love attention and to be cared for. I pay it back in kind with your charm and your ability to make people feel better when they’re blue.

Moon in Sagittarius

The Moon rules your emotions, moods, and feelings. This is likely the sign you most think of yourself as, since it reflects your personality when you’re alone or deeply comfortable. My Moon in Sagittarius, meaning my emotional self is restless, self-questioning, optimistic, and independent. I may hide my sensitivities behind an interest in finding new ways to feel.

Rising in Sagittarius

Your rising or ascendent is the "mask" you present to people. It can be seen in your personal style and how you come off to people when you first meet. Some say it becomes less relevant as you get older. My rising in Sagittarius, meaning I come across as independent, optimistic, and confident, though sometimes overly blunt or critical. Generally a charming conversationalist, my free-spritied approach may come off as restless or easily bored.

All of the information comes from CoStar, an app I use to check my daily updates and forecasts. If you are interested in horoscope now, you can download the app. All the information you need is your birthday, birthplace, and birth time.
