Jaspurr the White Chonky Kitty

Our Story

I have always been a crazy cat person, but my parents always thought cats are mean and lazy creatures that don’t appreciate love and affection. I get their point but I don’t think it is fair to not give love to cats just because they are not as affectionate as dogs. After months of persuasion, my parents finally agreed to take me to a cat adoption fair. That is when I fell in love with Jaspurr at first sight. He was sitting quietly in his cage, unlike other kittens who were jumping around asking for attentions and pets. He has beautiful blue eyes, and he was staring at me the whole time we were there. I knew I had to take him home.

His Catsonnality

Have you noticed the pun in his name yet? Jas-purr? Thank you. I think it is very creative too. Jaspurr is a very shy and gentle cat. He like to sleep in hidden corners and behind the furnitures so that no one can bother him when he gets his beauty sleep. However, at night he get his "crackhead hours" when he would jump around and cause chaos around the household. We can never get mad at him because he would purposely curl up on your feet. That gesture melts people's heart everytime.

Where Is He Now?

Over the summer, I went back home to bay area and saw Jaspurr at home. My parents had to go back to China to see their family, so I told them I will take care of Jaspurr in NYC. I flew back to the city with Jaspurr, and he has been living in my apartnment. He has been a great roommate, and even killed a cockroach for us.
